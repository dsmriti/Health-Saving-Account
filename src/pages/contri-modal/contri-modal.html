About Your Annual Contribution

Each year the Federal government determines the total maximum dollar amount both you and your employer may contribute. For 2017, this maximum is $3,400 if you are Single, and $6,750 for all other coverage levels.

If you are aged 55 years or older, you may also make "catch-up" contributions of up to $1,000 in addition to your respective maximum contribution amount regardless of coverage level.
