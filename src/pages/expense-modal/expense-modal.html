What expenses are eligible?

Funds withdrawn from your HSA can be used to pay for eligible medical expenses. Consider the following common expenses when estimating your annual withdrawals:

  • Deductible, Co-Pays, and/or Co-Insurance
  • Prescription drugs
  • Dental treatment
  • Glasses, contacts, and vision exams
  • Hearing exams and hearing aids
  • Chiropractic care
